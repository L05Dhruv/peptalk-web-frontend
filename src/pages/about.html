Meet our team

Digant Nanavati, MD Founder & CEO
Pep Talk Health was founded by Dr. Digant Nanavati, MD, a family medicine practitioner with over 25 years of experience in Central New York. Recognizing the gap in patient education and the challenges healthcare practices face in adopting digital tools, he created Pep Talk Health to bridge that divide. Our mission is to deliver affordable, effective solutions that integrate easily into your practice, empowering both you and your patients to make informed health decisions.
